# Privacy Policy — Favs Panel

_Last updated: 2026-07-08_

Favs Panel is a Chrome extension that helps you browse, search, and organize your Chrome bookmarks.

## Data collection

Favs Panel does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. It has no backend server, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. Nothing is ever sent over the network.

## How your data is used

- **Bookmarks** (`bookmarks` permission): read locally to display them inside the extension's panel, and to create or delete bookmarks you choose to manage from within the panel. This data never leaves your device.
- **Favicons** (`favicon` permission): used only to show each bookmarked site's icon next to its entry in the panel.
- **Preferences** (theme, language, view mode): stored locally in your browser via `localStorage`, only on your device.

No personal information, browsing history, location, authentication data, financial data, communications, or any other user data is collected, sold, or transferred to any third party.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be reflected on this page.
